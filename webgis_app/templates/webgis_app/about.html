About Us

Who We Are

We are a dynamic team of innovators—Ashim Gautam, Laxmi Acharya, and Pragya Joshi—united by a shared passion for leveraging technology to bridge the gap between geography and healthcare. With diverse expertise and a commitment to making a difference, we are dedicated to creating impactful solutions that empower communities through geospatial health insights.

Our Vision

To transform how healthcare resources are accessed by seamlessly integrating geospatial technology with healthcare systems. We envision a future where individuals can quickly locate nearby healthcare facilities within their preferred radius, enabling informed, timely, and life-saving decisions.

What We Do

Our app leverages cutting-edge geospatial technologies to simplify healthcare access. By allowing users to define a buffer radius, it identifies hospitals and healthcare facilities in their vicinity, providing real-time insights tailored to their specific needs. This empowers individuals to make proactive decisions for their health and well-being, bridging the gap between technology and healthcare.
